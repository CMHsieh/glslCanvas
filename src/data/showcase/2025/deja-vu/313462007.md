作品探索台灣與日本景觀的多頻率視覺疊合。使用 GLSL shader 實作「低頻 → 中頻 → 高頻」三層影像結構，並在網頁中提供四組可選圖片、可調節 Low、Mid、High 與 MidGain 的滑桿控制。透過距離、混合與視覺參數的互動，觀者可體驗圖像由模糊到細節的轉換與文化交錯。

The work explores a multi-frequency visual fusion of Taiwanese and Japanese landscapes. Implemented with a GLSL shader, it constructs a three-layer image structure of low, mid, and high frequencies. The webpage features four selectable image sets and adjustable sliders for Low, Mid, High, and MidGain. Through interactions of distance, blending, and visual parameters, viewers can experience the transformation of images from blur to detail, revealing a cross-cultural interplay of perception.
