This is a visual effect featuring a black cat, both inside and outside the frame. Inside the frame, there is a dynamic image of the cat segmented into numerous grids. Outside the frame, you can see the silhouette of the black cat. The interactive element allows you to restore the cat's appearance by hovering the mouse over it.
